Capabilities


LISTAR CAPABILITIES

┌──(kali@kali)-[~]
└─$ getcap -r / 2> /dev/null


ELIMINAR CAPABILITIES

┌──(kali@kali)-[~]
└─$ setcap -r /usr/bin/binario


ASIGNAR CAPABILITIES

┌──(kali@kali)-[~]
└─$ setcap cap_loquesea /usr/bin/binario
